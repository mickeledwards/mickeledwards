Website Build Process

I originally studied computer engineering. Throughout that, I developed both a methodological mindset and a love for creating things from scratch. With my need to control every last detail, I found that no readymade website builders catered towards my needs. Taking my previous experience in working with web design, I was able to find a good build system that works for me.

I am using a static site generator titled Jekyll. This allows me to take parts and pieces that I've written and collate them into fully working webpages without needing to rewrite common parts, like the footer or navigation bar.
